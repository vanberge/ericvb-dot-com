Two and Three-Quarters

Monday, February 22nd, 2010

Today we had our next check-up appointment.  We’re at the point where we go every two weeks, so time is really starting to fly. When the Dr. came in he asked Eric and I ” How are the two and a half of you doing?”  Then as we talked, we realized it’s more like two and three-quarters, of us since Baby Van is a few days from being 32 weeks old. If I go full term that only leaves us 8 more weeks!!

Things are slowly progressing in the baby room. At this point, we’re just waiting for the carpeting to come in and get installed so we can get the paint up, and get furniture moved into the room. I’m really hoping that the carpeting being here “within three weeks” ends up being less than two so that we can get moving. Although we still have a month before we hit that 36 week point, at which Baby Van would be okay entering the world, I’m getting nervous that we’re going to be scrambling. My goal is to have everything set in the room by March 31st. Somehow I think that date will be here before we know it.

I’m excited to start putting stuff in the room and seeing it all come together. We’ve got lots of things to start putting away, thanks to all our great friends and family who have helped stock us with all kinds of fun baby stuff – right now the basement looks like Babies R Us has moved in. It really makes Eric and I appreciate how many people care about us 🙂

Not much else to update right now – for those who are keeping track, the heartbeat today was right around 140.  We’ve mostly been in the 150s, with one other time where it was 136. So, basically Baby Van likes to keep us all guessing.

And finally, some more baby bump photos – the little guy/gal had definitely expanded his or her living quarters!

Ultrasound V2

Wednesday, February 10th, 2010

Finally “Dad” getting around to posting an update!

Today we were excited to have a second ultrasound – the premise of seeing how Baby Van  has developed and grown has been is something I think we’ve both looked forward to since the appointment was scheduled.  Normally I think people may just get one ultrasound at the 20 week mark; but Chris was measuring slightly below her doctor’s expectations.  So, they just wanted to do another ultrasound and measurements to verify due dates, growth rates, and everything else was still in line.

As expected, everything is indeed fine – her measurements are on track for our due date and the baby size rates at the 55th percentile.  The ultrasound was cool!  Baby Van is definitely more developed and has much less free space to move around in.  We still didn’t find out the boy/girl factor (sorry moms) – so that will still be a suprise on Baby Van’s Birthday…  But man it’s just crazy to me the level of detail you can see on the baby’s face/hands/feet with modern ultrasound technology.  Although we weren’t able to get a 3d rendering this time due to lack of  free space and positioning.

After the ultrasound, final checks with the doc revealed normal heartbeats, etc – so it seems everything is on track sailing smoothly.

Chris is now coming up to 30 weeks – so we kick into an appointment every two weeks during this 10 week home stretch…  Man, time sure is flying.  I’ve got a lot of drywall work and painting to do and the clock is ticking a little faster than I’d like to admit.  🙁

The ultrasound technician said that video recording of any ultrasound is prohibited – she seemed like she wouldn’t have been to much of a problem to convince otherwise (since she thought my Flip was a cell phone) but I decided I would follow established procedures.  So, bummer – no video.  Blah blah blah – enough talky.  Here are the ultrasound pics!

A Glimpse into the Room of Baby Van

Saturday, February 6th, 2010

We have officially moved into our “third trimester” which means we are getting closer to the debut of Baby Van. In the meantime, the mom- and dad-to-be are starting to pull things together to change what was the official junk room into a cute, inviting, and oh yeah – safe – place for the newest addition to reside.

I thought I’d take this chance to post a few images (some that are stock, some that are ours) of the things we have for the room so far. We went this week and made a decision on the paint color (Tiggers Tummy to be exact – a light, but bright yellow) and carpeting for the room. Now it’s just a matter of pulling it all together.

Also, we get to have another glimpse at the actual Baby Van this coming Wednesday. At my last appointment the doctor felt it would be a good idea to have another ultrasound since I’m measuring a little small for how far along I am. She seemed to think everything is fine, but they just want to take measurements of the baby to be sure. Both Eric and I are excited for the chance to see our little guy/gal on the ultrasound again, but also want to ensure everything is okay. Eric’s hoping to be able to take a video when we go, so we’ll try to post that. If anything, we should have some photos at least. And, for those who are wondering (the grandma’s especially :)), we are still not planning to find out the sex.  And no, we won’t have them just tell you.  So, look for that post later this week.

Onward to the fun stuff…images are below.
